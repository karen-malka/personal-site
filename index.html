My Design Process

I first identify what exactly I am creating and why: who is the audience? what are they looking for?

Then I research and explore what my client(s)/user(s) goals are: including noting past attempts at solutions, conducting user interviews, discovering the current market

Planning and Design are key aspects of the process where I create wireframes, sketches, and outlines with an emphasis on finding a balance between common design patterns and new creative aspects

Once I prepared the user interface, I enhance the user experience with prototyping where I build the mockup of the product, highlighting usability as top priority

The final step is testing where I ensure that my design is fit for the user(s)/client(s), and I analyze the results and repeat the process, if necessary!
