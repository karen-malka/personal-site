Art Alcove Website

Overview

This website was made to provide users with a way to discover different types of art materials that would most appropriately fit their desired art project.

Users type a query into the information retrieval system such as:

  • I want to paint a landscape using blue and green watercolors, cheap
  • I need high quality colored pencils and markers
  • I want to bead a bracelet for my best friend
  • I want to create a drawing with fountain pens but I only have $5

The IR system pulls the the art material data from Blick, an art site, and it determines its recommendations based on word matching with the query (using a technique called cosine similarity), a text mining aspect (specifically SVD) for interpretability, and product reviews.

Usability testing revealed an issue with the quotations on the Family Engagement page.

Initially, on the Family Engagement page, there was a sidebar of quotations. However, it seemed too cluttered and was not very usable. Thus, I created a carousel of quotes to put at the bottom of the page.
